07 / DOCTRINE

Rules of the grind

  • 01
    GPA is the floor, not the goal. 3.7+ keeps every door open. It is the cheapest edge you have — protect it first.
  • 02
    Follow up within 24 hours. Met someone, did an interview, got advice? Thank-you note the same day. This alone beats most peers.
  • 03
    Depth beats a long list. One leadership role and one placed case comp outweigh eight clubs you merely attended.
  • 04
    Ship proof, not plans. A public dashboard, a repo, a placed comp. Recruiters trust artifacts, not adjectives.
  • 05
    Referrals > cold apps. Find a Terry alum before you hit submit. One warm intro is worth fifty portals.
  • 06
    Rush both. Fully. You only get bids from rooms you show up to. Non-negotiable for the first two weeks.
